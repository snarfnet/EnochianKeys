Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is Enochian?
Enochian is a mystical language purportedly received by occultists John Dee and Edward Kelley in the 16th century. This app provides a dictionary, alphabet guide, and translator based on historical sources.

Q: How does the translation work?
The translator uses a curated word-by-word dictionary of Enochian vocabulary. Word-for-word translation is provided; fluent grammatical output may vary.
